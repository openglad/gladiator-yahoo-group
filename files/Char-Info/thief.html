Thief Character

This file contains comments about strategy for use of this character and suggested improvements for this character class.

Strategy

  • Lay a line of bombs by holding down the special key to injure groups of pursuing opponents

  • Lay a cluster of bombs down in a centralized location and circle the cluster; time your circling so that your opponents are on top of the cluster when the bombs go off

Improvements

  • Make skilled thieves automatically invisible when they are in the shadows next to walls

  • Let thieves pick or blow up locks

  • Let thieves lay down a fuse that they can ignite for the timed detonation of bombs

  • Make thieves invisible for a brief period if they walk under their own gas cloud

  • Give thieves a weaker version of the mage's teleport and the skeleton's burrow that lets them escape close combat
