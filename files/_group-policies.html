Group Policies

  • No announcements of any other message board are allowed without approval of the moderator.
  • All members are required to have a valid email address. (The moderator will not test for that unless a member starts getting undeliverable email.)
  • All new members are automatically moderated until the moderator is convinced said new member is not going to spam the list.
  • Don't ask for the password for Gladiator. (P.S. The password is IAMCHEAP)
  • No flames are allowed and the moderator has a fire truck on standby to deal with flames.
  • The moderator does not mind criticism as long as criticism does not become personal.
  • No attachements to messages are allowed.
  • Sending messages in HTML format is discouraged.
  • Posting announcements about a new group of scenarios is allowed/encouraged and the moderator loves to go through new scenarios.
  • Posting personal pictures in the group's photos section isn't allowed; we are reserving that space for screen captures of scenarios and the eventual display of maps of scenarios.
